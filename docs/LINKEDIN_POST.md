🚀 I built 16 Garments Production Calculators in one FREE web app — and it works offline too!

After spending years in the garments & apparel industry, I kept seeing the same problem:
Production managers pulling out phones, digging through spreadsheets, or doing mental math on the factory floor just to answer simple questions like:

❓ "What's our line efficiency today?"
❓ "How many meters of fabric do we need for 500 pieces?"
❓ "What's the SAM for this operation?"

So I built the solution. 👇

---

🧵 **OCS Garments Production Calculator Suite**
**16 professional calculators. One fast, beautiful app.**

Here's what's inside:

🏭 **PRODUCTION (5 tools)**
✅ Line Efficiency Calculator
✅ Line OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)
✅ Production Capacity Calculator
✅ Line SAH Calculator
✅ Hourly Production Target

📊 **EFFICIENCY (5 tools)**
✅ Operation SAM / SMV Calculator
✅ Individual Employee Efficiency
✅ Employee Efficiency (Multi-operator)
✅ Labour Productivity Calculator
✅ Machine Productivity Calculator

🔍 **QUALITY (1 tool)**
✅ Quality DHU Calculator

🧵 **FABRIC (5 tools)**
✅ T-Shirt Fabric Consumption
✅ Woven Shirt Fabric Consumption
✅ Kg → Meter Converter
✅ Meter → Kg Converter
✅ Kg → Yards Converter

---

🎨 **What makes it special?**

→ **Dark mode by default** — easy on the eyes during night shifts
→ **English + বাংলা** — bilingual for Bangladeshi factories
→ **Desktop & mobile** — inputs on the left, results on the right (no scrolling!)

→ **100% FREE & Open Source** — no login, no ads, no nonsense
→ **Instant results** — no server, runs entirely in the browser
