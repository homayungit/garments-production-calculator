🚀 I just built 16 FREE Garments Production Calculators in one app — dark mode, bilingual & works offline!

After years in the garments industry, I kept watching factory managers do mental math on the floor or dig through spreadsheets for answers to simple questions:

❓ "What's our line efficiency today?"
❓ "How many meters of fabric do we need for 500 pcs?"
❓ "What's the DHU this week?"

So I built the tool every garments professional deserves — and made it 100% FREE. 👇
